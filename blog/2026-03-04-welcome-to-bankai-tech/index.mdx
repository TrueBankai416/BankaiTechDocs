Welcome to the official Bankai-Tech blog! This is where I'll be sharing news, tutorials, deep-dives, and thoughts on all things self-hosting, Docker, and homelab infrastructure.

## Why Start a Blog?

After years of documenting various self-hosting solutions and Docker configurations, I realized that some content doesn't quite fit the documentation format. Sometimes you want to:

- **Share thoughts and opinions** on new tools and technologies
- **Discuss trends** in the self-hosting community
- **Provide quick tips** that don't warrant a full tutorial
- **Announce updates** to the documentation site
- **Document my homelab journey** and lessons learned

## What to Expect

Here's what you can look forward to in future posts:

### Technical Deep-Dives
Detailed explorations of specific technologies, comparing different approaches, and sharing real-world experiences running services in production.

### Quick Tips & Tricks
Short, actionable tips that can improve your homelab setup without requiring a full tutorial read.

### News & Updates
Announcements about new documentation, major updates to tools we cover, and community news.

### Homelab Stories
Real experiences from running a homelab - the wins, the fails, and everything in between.
